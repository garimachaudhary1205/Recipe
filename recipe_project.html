Gulab Jamun Recipe

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cup Maida (All Purpose Flour)
  2. 1 cup grated Mawa (Khoya)(approx. 200-225 gms)
  3. 1/8 teaspoon Baking Soda
  4. Ghee (or oil), for deep frying
  5. 3-4 Green Cardamoms or 1/4 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi Powder)
  6. 8-10 Saffron Strands (kesar)
  7. 1½ cups Sugar
  8. 2½ cups Water


directions

Boil sugar and water to prepare syrup remove from fire. Add cardamom and keep aside to cool. In a mixing bowl put the flour, paneer, sooji, Nestlé MILKMAID, baking powder and baking soda. Mix gently to make a soft dough. Do not knead too much. Divide the mixture into 30-35 portions and gently roll into round gulab jamuns. Fry in the oil at very low flame till golden brown in color. Keep putting the fried gulab jamuns in the prepared sugar syrup. Once all the gulab jamuns are in the sugar syrup bring it to a boil and remove from flame. Serve warm.

Kaju Katli Recipe

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups (250g) whole raw cashew nuts
  2. 2 tablespoons (15g) whole-milk powder
  3. 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  4. 10 tablespoons plus 1 1/2 teaspoons (125g) granulated sugar
  5. 1/4 cup (60ml) water
  6. Rounded 1/2 teaspoon ghee
  7. Edible silver leaf or vark, optional


Directions

Blend cashews and milk in a blender to a fine paste. Mix paste and sugar and cook over low heat stirring till the sugar dissolves, then bring to a boil. Continue stirring over medium heat, till the mixture leaves the sides of the pan, and becomes a dough like paste. Remove from heat and when cool enough to handle, roll it on to a greased surface, with a greased rolling pin (roll before it cools). Roll to 1/4cm / 1/8" thickness. Now cover with the silver leaf and leave to cool, then cut into diamond shaped pieces.
